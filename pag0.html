Diet Plan for Weight Loss

Time Meal Suggested Food Items
Breakfast Oatmeal with Fresh Fruit Oats, almond milk, banana, blueberries, chia seeds
Mid-Morning Snack Mixed Nuts and Seeds Almonds, walnuts, sunflower seeds, pumpkin seeds
Lunch Grilled Chicken Salad Grilled chicken breast, mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumbers, olive oil dressing
Afternoon Snack Greek Yogurt with Honey Low-fat Greek yogurt, honey, a handful of mixed berries
Dinner Baked Salmon with Quinoa and Vegetables Baked salmon, quinoa, steamed broccoli, roasted carrots
Evening Snack Herbal Tea with a Small Piece of Dark Chocolate Herbal tea, 1-2 squares of dark chocolate (70% cocoa or higher)

Note: This is a sample diet plan. Please consult with a healthcare professional or a registered dietitian for personalized dietary recommendations based on your individual health needs.
