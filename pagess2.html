Diet Plan for Weight Loss in Thyroid for Obese Individuals

Time Meal Suggested Food Items
Breakfast Greek Yogurt with Flaxseeds and Berries Low-fat Greek yogurt, flaxseeds, blueberries, strawberries
Mid-Morning Snack Green Smoothie Spinach, kale, banana, almond milk, chia seeds
Lunch Grilled Salmon Salad Grilled salmon, mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumbers, avocado, olive oil dressing
Afternoon Snack Nuts and Seeds Mix Almonds, walnuts, pumpkin seeds
Dinner Quinoa with Steamed Vegetables Quinoa, steamed broccoli, cauliflower, carrots, grilled chicken breast
Evening Snack Herbal Tea with a Small Piece of Dark Chocolate Herbal tea (such as chamomile or peppermint), 1-2 squares of dark chocolate (70% cocoa or higher)

Note: This is a sample diet plan. Please consult with a healthcare professional or a registered dietitian for personalized dietary recommendations based on your individual health needs.
