Diet Plan for Weight Loss in Diabetes (Overweight)

Time Meal Suggested Food Items
Breakfast Vegetable Omelette Egg whites, spinach, tomatoes, mushrooms
Mid-Morning Snack Greek Yogurt with Berries Low-fat Greek yogurt, mixed berries
Lunch Grilled Chicken Salad Grilled chicken breast, mixed greens, cucumbers, bell peppers, olive oil dressing
Afternoon Snack Raw Almonds Handful of raw almonds
Dinner Baked Fish with Steamed Vegetables Baked fish (such as cod or salmon), broccoli, cauliflower, carrots

Note: This is a sample diet plan. Please consult with a healthcare professional or a registered dietitian for personalized dietary recommendations based on your individual health needs.
