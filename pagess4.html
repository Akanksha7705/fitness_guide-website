Diet Plan for Weight Loss in Hypotension for Obese Individuals

Time Meal Suggested Food Items
Breakfast Scrambled Eggs with Whole-Grain Toast Eggs, whole-grain bread, avocado, cherry tomatoes
Mid-Morning Snack Mixed Nuts and Dried Fruits Almonds, walnuts, dried apricots, raisins
Lunch Grilled Chicken and Quinoa Salad Grilled chicken breast, quinoa, mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumbers, olive oil dressing
Afternoon Snack Greek Yogurt with Honey and Berries Low-fat Greek yogurt, honey, mixed berries
Dinner Baked Salmon with Steamed Vegetables Baked salmon, quinoa, steamed broccoli, roasted carrots
Evening Snack Herbal Tea with a Small Piece of Dark Chocolate Herbal tea (such as chamomile or peppermint), 1-2 squares of dark chocolate (70% cocoa or higher)

Note: This is a sample diet plan. Please consult with a healthcare professional or a registered dietitian for personalized dietary recommendations based on your individual health needs.
